## CTA optimisation

Every video must include:
1. **In the first 30 seconds**: "If you want live NATA coaching, link in description goes to neramclasses.com/nata-online-coaching"
2. **At the midpoint (around minute 4 to 5)**: "Quick reminder, the link below also has our free cutoff calculator"
3. **In the last 60 seconds**: End-screen with subscribe button + link to next video in the playlist
4. **Pinned comment**: Top 3 free resource links

## Thumbnail style

- High contrast: dark background, bright yellow or red text
- Title visible at 120x67 thumbnail size (the YouTube search-list size)
- Faculty face on the right side (builds trust)
- Day number in a circle (e.g. "Day 4")
- NATA 2026 stamp in corner
