## Follow-up cadence

- Initial email: Day 0
- First follow-up: Day 7 (if no response)
- Second follow-up: Day 21 (if still no response)
- Move on after 2 follow-ups

## Personalisation rules

1. Always use the editor or institute manager's name. Avoid "Hi team" for editorial pitches.
2. Reference one specific thing from their recent content (a published article, an existing institute they reviewed).
3. Keep the email under 250 words. Long emails get archived.
4. Include 2 to 3 verifiable links (not 10). Quality over quantity.
5. Avoid generic claims. "10,000 students" is fine because it is verifiable; "best in India" gets flagged as marketing copy.
