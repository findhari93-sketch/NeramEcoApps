## Per-centre claim checklist

For each centre, complete every item before moving to the next:

### Basic profile (claim phase)
- [ ] Verify centre via postcard, video call, or phone (GBP options vary by region)
- [ ] Confirm business name format: "Neram Classes, [City]" or "Neram Classes [Area]"
- [ ] Set primary category to "Coaching Centre" or "Educational Consultant"
- [ ] Add secondary category: "Architecture School" (only if CoA-approved)
- [ ] Confirm address with pincode and landmark
- [ ] Confirm phone number (use a city-specific landline if available)
- [ ] Add website URL with UTM tracking: `https://neramclasses.com/nata-coaching/[CITY]?utm_source=gbp&utm_medium=organic`
- [ ] Set service area (radius around the centre, typically 25 km)

### Content (optimisation phase)
- [ ] Add a 750-character business description that includes "NATA online coaching in [CITY]" naturally
- [ ] Upload 10+ photos:
  - 2 exterior photos
  - 3 interior photos (classroom, drawing studio, reception)
  - 2 faculty photos
  - 2 batch-in-session photos (with consent)
  - 1 logo photo
- [ ] Upload 1 short video (30 to 60 seconds) showing the centre
- [ ] Set hours: typically Mon to Sat, 9 AM to 6 PM (verify per centre)
- [ ] Add attributes: "Free Wi-Fi", "Wheelchair-accessible entrance", "LGBTQ+ friendly", "Identifies as women-owned" (where applicable)

### Services and products
- [ ] Add the 3 NATA programs as "Services":
  - NATA Crash Course (Rs. 15,000)
  - 1-Year NATA Program (Rs. 25,000)
  - 2-Year NATA + JEE Paper 2 Program (Rs. 30,000)
- [ ] Each service must have a description and a price (transparent fees are a differentiator)
- [ ] Add JEE Paper 2 Coaching as a separate service

### Posts and updates (ongoing)
- [ ] Post 1 update per week per centre. Rotate themes:
  - Week 1: Free demo class invite
  - Week 2: Faculty profile spotlight
  - Week 3: Recent topper story (with consent)
  - Week 4: Upcoming batch start date
- [ ] Each post should be 100 to 300 words with 1 photo
- [ ] Include a call-to-action button: "Book", "Call now", "Learn more"

### Reviews
- [ ] Set up a review-request workflow: every enrolled student gets a Google review request after their first month
- [ ] Use a polite, no-pressure script: "If Neram has helped, a Google review helps other students find us."
- [ ] Never offer incentives for reviews (against Google guidelines)
- [ ] Target: 25+ reviews per centre by week 12, average rating 4.6+
- [ ] Respond to every review (positive and negative) within 48 hours

### Questions and answers
- [ ] Pre-populate the GBP Q&A section with 8 to 10 frequently asked questions and your answers
- [ ] Examples:
  - "What is the fee for NATA coaching at Neram?"
  - "Do you offer online classes from [CITY]?"
  - "What is the batch size?"
  - "Can I switch from online to offline mid-course?"
- [ ] Monitor for new questions and respond within 24 hours

## What to avoid

1. **Fake reviews.** Google detects and removes these, plus risks suspension.
2. **Multiple GBP profiles for the same centre.** One profile per address only.
3. **Stuffing the business name with keywords.** "Neram Classes Best NATA Coaching Bangalore" is a violation. Just use "Neram Classes, Bangalore".
4. **Inconsistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) across the web.** Whatever you set in GBP must match your website footer, Shiksha listing, Collegedunia listing, etc.
5. **Skipping the verification step.** Unverified profiles never appear in Map Pack.
6. **Letting the profile go stale.** No posts for 60+ days hurts ranking.
