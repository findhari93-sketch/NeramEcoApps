## Tracking

Maintain a weekly tracker in Notion or a Google Sheet with these columns:
- Channel name
- Outreach sent date
- Response date (or N/A)
- Backlink landed: yes/no
- Estimated DA of placement
- Notes

Target: 40 new referring domains by week 12 (per the SEO plan).
