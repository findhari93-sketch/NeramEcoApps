# NATA 2026 Crash Course — Complete Course Plan

## Course Overview

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Duration** | 5 weeks (30 teaching days) |
| **Schedule** | Tuesday to Sunday, Monday off |
| **Sessions** | Morning 11:00–12:00 + Evening 7:00–8:00 |
| **Total Sessions** | 60 (30 days × 2 per day) |
| **Total Hours** | 60 hours |
| **Mode** | Online via Microsoft Teams |
| **Course completes first** | Students attempt NATA after Week 5 |

| Teacher | Slot | Subjects |
|---------|------|----------|

| **Sivaram** | Evening (7–8) | Math (geometry, mensuration, ratios, percentages, profit/loss), clock & mirror problems, direction sense, blood relations, number series, probability |
