Key Design Decisions

With Teacher Reference

Side-by-side on desktop. Swipe toggle (Student | Reference) on mobile. Purple badge marks teacher content.

Without Teacher Reference

Full-width single image. No toggle needed. Card adapts automatically based on available data.

Filter: Teacher Refs Only

Toggle at top filters to only show posts that have teacher references. Shows question + teacher image for study.

Load More (not infinite scroll)

Explicit "Load More" button. Students control when to load. Saves bandwidth on mobile. 12 posts per batch.
