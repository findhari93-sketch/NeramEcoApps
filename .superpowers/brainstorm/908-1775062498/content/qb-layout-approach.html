How should the Question Bank be structured when a student opens it?

A

Filter-First (like Amazon product search)

Left sidebar with powerful hierarchical filters (Subject > Chapter > Topic). All 624 questions load in a scrollable list on the right. Filters narrow down the list in real-time. No exam/year/session navigation needed upfront.

Compact filter bar at the top with dropdowns (Subject, Chapter, Difficulty, Exam, Status). Full question list below with no sidebar. Maximizes question viewing area. Filter chips show active filters.
