College Card Tier Layout

How should top colleges vs. rest be displayed? Pick the layout that feels right.

A

Top 10 Full-Width + Rest 2-Column

Top 10 colleges get a large, detailed horizontal card (campus photo left, full info right). After #10, switch to a compact 2-column grid. Ad banners appear every 5 cards.

B

Top 5 Featured + Rest Single Column

Top 5 colleges get an extra-large "featured" card with campus photo, stats, and highlights. Remaining colleges all get single-column cards (smaller but still one-per-row). Ad banners between groups of 5.

C

Ranked List (Shiksha-style)

All colleges in a numbered vertical list, one per row. Top 10 have a prominent rank badge and expanded info. Rest are compact rows. Rank numbers prominent on the left. Ad banners every 5 rows.
