📱 Mobile UX Patterns

1. Floating Filter + Sort Buttons
Two floating pills at bottom center. "Filters" shows active count badge. "Sort" opens a bottom sheet with sort options. Always visible while scrolling.
2. Horizontal Quick-Filter Chips
Scrollable chip bar below search. Tap a chip to toggle or open a mini bottom-sheet for that filter. Shows selected state with blue highlight.
3. Full-Screen Filter Drawer
Tapping the floating "Filters" button opens an 85%-height bottom drawer with all filter options. Includes search, checkboxes, chips. Sticky "Apply" button at bottom. 48px+ touch targets.
4. Featured Cards: Stacked Vertical
Top 5 colleges get full-width cards with 150px campus photo, logo overlay, 3-column stats grid, and action buttons. Thumb-friendly zone for CTA buttons.
5. Compact Cards: Tap to Navigate
Remaining colleges in horizontal row cards. Rank + logo on left, info in middle, chevron on right. Entire card is tappable to go to detail page. Shows fee + seats inline.
6. Ad Banners: Native-Feel
Subtle sponsored cards blend with the listing. Appear after every 3-5 colleges. Labeled "Sponsored" clearly. Touch-friendly CTA.
