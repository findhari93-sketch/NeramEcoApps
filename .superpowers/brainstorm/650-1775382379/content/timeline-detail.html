Key Differences by Role

Student sees

  • Their own exam dates highlighted
  • Peers writing on the same dates
  • "Not submitted" banner (if they haven't submitted)
  • FAB to add/update their exam date
  • Recall nudge after their exam completes

Teacher additionally sees

  • Full "not submitted" student list with reminder buttons
  • "Send Reminder to All" bulk action
  • "+ Add Exam Date" to create new dates
  • All students across all dates (not just peers)
  • Recently completed section with all students
