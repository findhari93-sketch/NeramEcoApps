NATA Exam Schedule Dashboard - Layout Approaches

Which layout structure feels right for organizing upcoming exams, student status, and follow-ups?

A

Timeline View (Recommended)

Vertical timeline of upcoming exam dates (nearest first). Each date card expands to show grouped students by city. Top banner shows "Not Submitted" count with expandable list. Bottom section for "Recently Completed" with recall nudge.

B

Calendar Grid View

Weekly calendar showing Fri/Sat columns with student avatars/chips stacked on each date. Sidebar shows "Not Submitted" and "Recently Completed" lists. More visual but may get crowded with many students.

C

Tab-Based Sections

Three tabs: "Upcoming" (list of dates with students), "Not Submitted" (students without dates), "Completed" (post-exam follow-up). Clean separation but requires tab switching to see the full picture.

Trade-offs

Approach Best for Weakness
A. Timeline Quick scanning, mobile-friendly, shows everything on one scroll Less visual than calendar
B. Calendar Visual date awareness, familiar pattern Harder on mobile, wasted space on non-exam days
C. Tabs Clean separation, good for large lists Can't see upcoming + not submitted at same time
